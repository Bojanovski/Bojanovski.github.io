About

Starting my 'career' at the age of ten when I wrote my first program in Logo I moved, driven by the burning desire to make a video game, to C#. After making a couple of games and game engines, I switched to C++ which remains my primary language to this day. Over the years, I completed my MS in computer science, worked as a software engineer in game development and media production industry. Also, I became an avid python programmer and I'm always on the lookout for excuses to use it. Currently, I work on low-level graphics programming and driver optimization for a big electronics company.
